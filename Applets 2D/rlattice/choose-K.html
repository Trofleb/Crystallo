Choosing a value of K

The points of the new reciprocal lattice fall along a perpendicular vector to these lines through our base point. The distance to a new lattice point along this line is K/D, where D is the measured distance between the lines we just created. K, is an arbitrary constant. You'll be given a scroll bar to help set the value of K the first time points on our new lattice are created.

(You may opt to simply keep default value presented to you, of course.)
