Introduction

The aim of this applet is to derive the reciprocal vectors of a periodic object in two dimensions. The reciprocal vectors are defined according to the following scalar product :

ai aj*=δij

where ai is the lattice vector, aj* is a reciprocal vector, δij=0 if i≠j and δij=1 if i=j
