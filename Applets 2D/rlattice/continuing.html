Continuing with the calculations

Continue the process of selecting new series of parallel lines, measuring the distance between them, and calculating a new series points along the normal to the series of lines at distances K/d.

After completing a few lines of points, click on the check button labelled «Show Original Lattice» and try to find the property of the series of points which have been created. By generating more and more points, a pattern should appear which is indeed the reciprocal lattice corresponding to your original pattern.
